Common questions

Everything you need to know

Do I need to create an account or subscribe?
No account. No subscription. No backend. You get a single HTML file. Open it in your browser and everything works immediately. All your data is stored locally in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes. It's fully responsive and works on Android, iPhone, tablet, desktop — any device with a modern browser. Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are all supported.
Are the job results real and up-to-date?
Yes. The app fetches live data directly from the APIs and RSS feeds of the actual job boards in real time. Every scan pulls the freshest available listings. The auto-scan keeps results updated every 15 minutes while the app is open.
Can I use it in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, or anywhere?
Absolutely. The currency support was specifically built for global job seekers. You can filter results to NGN, GHS, KES, ZAR, AED, PHP, INR and 77 other currencies. The app works from any country with internet access.
What happens if I lose the file?
Your resume, tracker data, and bookmarks are all saved in your browser's localStorage. If you lose the HTML file itself, just re-download it — your data stays in the browser. The Data Backup feature also lets you export everything to a JSON file for safekeeping.
What's the difference between Starter and Pro?
The app itself is identical in both. Pro includes all future updates (new job sources, features, bug fixes) at no extra cost, priority support via Instagram, and the Chrome Extension. Starter is a one-time purchase of the current version.
Can I customise it or resell it?
The Developer tier includes a commercial use license allowing you to customise and resell the tool. Starter and Pro are for personal use only.
